## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register your species">
    Navigate to **Species** and create fighters with a name, power level, and special ability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run a battle">
    Go to **Battles**, search for two combatants, and click **Fight** — or use **Random Battle** to let the server choose.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the ranking">
    Open **Rankings** to see which species has the most victories across all battles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor the dashboard">
    The **Dashboard** overview shows your total species count, the top-3 champions, and the most recent battle outcome at a glance.
  </Step>
</Steps>
